Overview

Experience Goal

Taking on the adventure as Sebastian the Bird, players will have a rush of excitement and wonder as they engage with their surroundings, tapping into the unique experience of using their beak and flying in the skies. However, as the Rainstorm approaches, players must remain vigilance, ensuring the protection of fellow animals from lurking predators. In the face of potential danger, players must also confront their own fears of becoming prey, and maintain courage in the challenges that lie ahead.

Other Levels

Design of Area 3-2

In Area 3-2, baby suqirrels are trapped on a lonely rock surrounded by alligators. Sebastian needs to find a way to reach the squirrels without noticed by gators. Though the heavy wind is not supposed for fly, luckily these gators have poor eyesight. Can Sebastian successfully sneak in and out? Be careful and keep in mind that water is their home field.

Design of Area 4-2

When the rain began to pour, Sebastian and squirrel family seek shelter under cliff. Though they are safe from predators, the ground is shaken by the landslide. Good thing is no need to hurry here, take your time , be patient, step by step, beak by beak. Find out the steady path or wait for the inbetween of gusts, and take the jump.
